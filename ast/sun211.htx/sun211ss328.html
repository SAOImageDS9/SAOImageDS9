LatAxis

Index of the latitude axis

Description:

This read-only attribute gives the index (1 or 2) of the latitude axis within the SkyFrame (taking into account any current axis permutations).
Type:
Integer.

Applicability

SkyFrame
All SkyFrames have this attribute.
